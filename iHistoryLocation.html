History & Location

The History of Café Illustre

Café Illustre was a dream come true. It was a brainchild of Mrs. Jennifer L. Tan, through a long, passionate and dedicative journey; December 2016, a dream was put into life and thus Café Illustre was born. It offers a wide array of the best cuisine that was prepared with a genuine passion of cooking. Today, Café Illustre continues to transform and evolve. From a mere food ordering business to the current catering service that serves for events that caters to a wide variety of people. As time passed, its cuisine has grown in numbers satisfying the changing taste, need, and interest of its customers. Through a great feedback mechanism, the restaurant was able to employ changes that will further improve the café moreover, with its established identity. The customers slowly grew through word of mouth.


Location of Café Illustre

#9 V. Ilustre Street, Barangay Don Manuel, Quezon City
