Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Do you have food packages?

A: We offer food packages depending on your need.

Q: What are your best sellers?

A: Kare-Kare, Caldereta, and Buttered Crabs.

Q: What are your modes of payment?

A: 50% Downpayment, 50% Upon delivery. Cash Basis or Bank Transaction.

Q: Do you deliver?

A: For a min. of 3,000php, we have free delivery in some parts of Metro Manila (delivery charge may vary with the area).

Q: How do we send our job applications?

A: Fill in the form and attach your resumé then click the submit button on the "Job Application" page.

Q: How do we attach our résumé?

A: Click on the "Upload Résumé" button. A window will pop up. Locate your file in the directory of where you've saved it and then click ok.

Q: When can we send our job applications?

A: You can send your job applications any time you want as long as the position is open.
